Let's get started

Creating a new portfolio enables you to organize all of your JEDI cloud applications, funding information, and reporting in one place. To get started, you’ll need to begin preparing your first task order, which will eventually be submitted in your organization’s system of record. If you’re unable to complete the task order draft in one session, any information entered will be saved for you to continue with when ready. You'll be guided through the necessary steps including:

  • Getting a statement of work
  • Providing market research
  • Completing security documentation
  • Obtaining approvals
